IRIS FLOWER CLASSIFICATION

The **Iris Flower Classification** is a popular machine learning task where the goal is to classify iris flowers into three species: **Setosa**, **Versicolor**, and **Virginica**. The classification is based on four features: **sepal length**, **sepal width**, **petal length**, and **petal width**. The dataset has 150 samples, with each flower's measurements recorded, and it's often used to train and evaluate machine learning models such as Logistic Regression, K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), or Decision Trees.
